INVESTMENT_VORTEXINTRO‘Investment vortex’ is a cooperative &amp; confrontation board game attract investment. featuring a modern world where monsters control capital and set up a large ‘roulette wheel’ provide employees compete for investment. Players act as employees for different companies competing for investment.The employees on the roulette wheel try to meet the conditions of the monsters.And get the monsters to invest at the right time. Layout page including intuitive final works GAMERULE INTERPRETATION‘INVESTMENT_VORTEX’ with it’s own special &amp; complexity game mechanics will be interpretated below.Game rules: The player selected to start with any rule (e.g. the youngest as the first player…) The game is played counterclockwise. Players must perform one of the following three actions during their turn: ACTION Card judgment: The player selects the psychologically anticipated card and the action card of his hand in the card slot within the range of the roulette wheel for judgment If the pattern is the same, replace the psychological anticipation cards in the card slot with action cards, and the psychological anticipation cards enter the hand. If different patterns correspond to the same color, the action card is withdrawn and the psychological expectation card is publicized If different patterns correspond to different colors, the action card is discarded and the psychological expectation card is publicized Insert cards: .. The player inserts the expected cards in his hand into any slot of the roulette wheel during the turn (no distance limit). .. A maximum of five cards can be placed in each card slot .. If there is an investment mark above the card slot, psychological expectation cards can be inserted Game Over: When all the psychological expectation cards under the investment marker have been completely replaced with action cards, the last player to complete all the replacements can claim the corresponding investment marker. The marker can be placed in an empty slot.When a player claims an investment marker and there are empty slots within their range, they can place the marker to occupy the slot and receive an investment ticket. If a player receives an investment token when the wheel is about to land in a state that is already occupied, the player cannot place their own investment token, and the wheel will continue to spin. If all the areas on the second wheel are occupied, the game ends, and the player who has occupied the most areas wins. VIDEOinterpretation video below to better express my comprehensive thoughts.
